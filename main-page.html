Cafe Afnan is a new pop-up cafe for coffee and matcha lovers. As a Muslim-owned cafe and pop-up, we bring you the warmth of home through rich, cultural flavors and carefully crafted creations. Inspired by the spices and traditions that make every sip feel like a hug, our drinks and treats blend comfort with adventure. Whether you're here for a quiet moment, a lively conversation, or a new favorite flavor, we invite you to experience drinks the way they were meant to be, full of heart.

What is a Pop-Up Cafe?

A pop-up cafe is a temporary, mobile coffee shop that brings high-quality drinks and unique experiences to different locations. Unlike traditional cafes, pop-ups dont have a permanent storefront, instead, they appear at special events, markets, and private gatherings for a limited time.

At Cafe Afnan, our pop-up allows us to share our love for coffee and matcha in a flexible and exciting way. We bring our signature drinks and warm atmosphere to you, wherever you are!
