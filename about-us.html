Our Inspiration

Afnan means full spreading branches of trees in Arabic. It is used in the Quran in Surah Ar- Rahman when the trees in the gardens of Jannah (heaven) are being described. "But for he who has feared the position of his Lord are two gardens - So which of the favors of your Lord would you deny? - With spreading branches [Afnan]." (Quran 55:46-48)
